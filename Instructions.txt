Instructions


## Keyboard press
Press 'a' for start time travel for the actor;
Press 'd' to step backward for the actor;
Press 's' to stop actor;
Press 'r' to reset the tank;
Press 't' to stop the tank;

## Mouse click
Scene-1
Click 'Left' Button to speed up army;
Click 'Right' Button to speed up tank;

Scene-2

Click 'Left' Button to move ships to shore;
Click 'Right' Button to move ships away;
